Pancake Recipe

These fluffy pancakes are perfect for breakfast and easy to make with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour a small amount of batter.
  4. Cook until golden on both sides and serve warm.
